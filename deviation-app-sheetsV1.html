A
Approver Configuration
Configure your default approver list — these are the people who will be pre-selected on every new deviation. Use the dropdown to set the default behavior for each:
• 🔒 Required — must sign for the deviation to pass
• 🔓 Optional — may sign but not required
• ⚡ Rule-Out — a single approval from this person passes the deviation regardless of others
The defaults apply to every new deviation but can be changed individually under Section 6 — Approval Configuration when creating one.
